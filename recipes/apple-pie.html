Apple Pie

Description

Take a bunch of apples. Slice em up. Put them in a pan

That‘s how easy it is to make something this delicious

Ingredients

  • 3 Apples
  • 4 Sticks of butter
  • 2 truck loads of sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 550F
  2. Peel apples
  3. Melt sugar with some cinamon
  4. Once caramel like consistency add apples
  5. Stick the mixture into a pie crust and then into the oven
  6. Bake until looks ready
