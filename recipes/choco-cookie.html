Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Crispy chocolate chip cookies with a gooey middle. Perfect for anyone with a sweet tooth and love for chocolate.

Ingredients

  • Dark Chocolate Chips
  • Flour
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Incorporate all dry ingredients
  3. Slowly add in the wet ingredients
  4. Litter with chocolate chips
  5. Put in the oven for 10-12 minutes
  6. Rest for 5 before eating
